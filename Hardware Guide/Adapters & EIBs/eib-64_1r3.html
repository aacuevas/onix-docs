eib-64 v1.3 is a 64-channel electrode interface board (EIB) with electrical and optical stimulation. It is designed for small animal (e.g. mouse) tetrode electrophysiology and is compatible with headstage-64 v1.3 and is designed to be used with the mouse variant of the shuttle-drive.
