This site provides documentation for ONIX (Open Neuro Interface X), the second-generation Open Ephys acquisition system. ONIX is a collection of Open Neuro Interface-compliant tools for performing neuroscience experiments with freely moving animals. ONIX is the successor to original Open Ephys Acquisition System and significantly expands on its scope, performance, and compatibility with other open-source tools. Here you can find

  • Technical information on ONIX hardware, programming interfaces, and software integration

  • Hardware and Software Manuals

  • Tutorials

  • ONIX-specific knowledge and hacks

For general information on Ephys techniques, have a look at the Open Ephys Wiki
